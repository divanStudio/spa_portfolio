The amazing flash game

Description

HeliBomber is the military version of popular game HeliCrane. You have to take personal control of powerful helicopter armed with really deadly weapons. Use rapidly rotating rotor of copter and all the available calibers to destroy the car of terrorists. Avoid enemy reaches the destination. The game contains complex obstacles, massed fire and pressing speed of the car. Earn as much as possible coins and bonuses from the accurate shoots. The pursuit is happening in front of the post-apocalyptical countryside background and is decorated with realistic physics.

Technologies
Adobe Flash, ActionScript 3, Box2D, Greensock TweenLite.
