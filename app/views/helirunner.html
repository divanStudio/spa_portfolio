HeliRunner

Android game

Description

Feel yourself like a pilot of powerful helicopter-crane! Use rapidly rotating rotor of copter and strong chain to help rescue car to reach the destination. Provide a freeway from point A to point B to save the lives. There are complex obstacles, tortuous routes, pressing speed of car and important secondary aim for progress. The pursuit is happening in front of the beautiful countryside background and is decorated with realistic physics. So finally, now it's the best time to try it!

Game features:

  • 12 levels with different kinds of obstacles
  • 5 types of helicopters
  • 3 types of rescue cars
  • system of earning coins and bonuses
  • different beautiful countryside
  • realistic physics
  • saving of users progress
Technologies
Adobe Flash, ActionScript 3, Box2D, Greensock TweenLite, Google Analytics.
